Rules of the game:

  • The "15 puzzle" is a sliding square puzzle;
  • Reposition the squares one at a time into the configuration shown before;
  • Arrange the tiles from number one to fifteen;
  • Move tiles up and down, to left or to the right toward the empty tile;
  • Tiles cannot be removed;
  • Enjoy playing the game.
